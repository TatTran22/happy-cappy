# Happy Cappy Manual Smoke Checklist

- Launch `dist/Happy Cappy.app`.
- Confirm there is no Dock or Cmd+Tab entry.
- Confirm the menu bar item title is `HC`.
- Open Settings from the available settings entry.
- Change personality to Calm, Cheerful, and Lively; confirm hover animation changes.
- Drag Happy Cappy to a new location.
- Quit and relaunch; confirm position persists.
- Right-click visible pet pixels; confirm context menu appears.
- Use Hide Pet; confirm pet disappears and menu bar app remains.
- Use Show Pet; confirm pet returns.
- Use Reset Position; confirm pet returns to a visible safe position.
- Open a window behind Happy Cappy and check transparent area behavior.
- Note whether transparent area pass-through is per-pixel or full-frame fallback.
- Enable Focus Mode from the menu bar; confirm clicks pass through the pet while it remains visible.
- Disable Focus Mode from the menu bar; confirm hover, drag, and right-click work again.
- Trigger Nap; confirm the pet switches to a sleepy expression and stops walking temporarily.
- Trigger Cheer Up; confirm the pet switches to a happy expression temporarily.
